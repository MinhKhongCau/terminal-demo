01 / Story

Built inside an old freight house.

The Terminal occupies a small brick building at the end of

a forgotten railway line — once used to stage parcels for the

morning train, now used to stage espresso and breakfast plates.

We borrow from the slowness of the old timetable. Our menu

is short, our hours are kept, and the coffee is pulled by hand.
